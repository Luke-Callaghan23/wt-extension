## First click here to open the imports folder.  This is where all documents you'd like to import should be stored.  Dump everything you want to import here.

![hr](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luke-Callaghan23/wt-extension/master/media/walkthroughs/imports/hr.png)

![overview_refresh](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luke-Callaghan23/wt-extension/master/media/walkthroughs/imports/overview_refresh.png)

## Once all imports are added, hit the refresh button to add them to the GUI.

![hr](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luke-Callaghan23/wt-extension/master/media/walkthroughs/imports/hr.png)

![overview_import](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luke-Callaghan23/wt-extension/master/media/walkthroughs/imports/overview_import_all.png)
